Iloilo City Jeepney Route Map

Interactive map showing all 25 official jeepney routes • Data source: LTFRB MC No. 2023-036

About this map

Routes follow actual streets via OpenStreetMap routing. Street names from ilonggoengineer.com (LTFRB MC No. 2023-036). Routes are cached after first load. Click ✏ Edit on any route to drag its waypoints, drag the route handle to shift the whole path, click the line to add one, or right-click a waypoint to delete it.

Edit mode: drag markers • drag route handle to shift whole path • click line to add waypoint • right-click marker to delete
